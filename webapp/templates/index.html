Tweet Guard

In digital times, digital crimes are also rocketing. People need a way to keep themselves safe from online threats. This application will warn you whenever a “threatening account” tries to interact with you. The scan function can be used in two different ways:

  • You can search for a specific account and receive a full report on the account.
  • You can enter your own Twitter handle and be given analysis of all of your followers under a selected criteria on which you choose to search, documented as a full report which highlights the dangerous users, and, if possible, will give an option to unfollow a specific user or mass unfollow all threatening accounts.
